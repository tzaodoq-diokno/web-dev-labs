Sip and Run Café

Welcome to Sip and Run Café, the perfect place where coffee meets the runner’s lifestyle. Whether you’re here for a warm drink, a quick snack, or gear to keep you moving, we’ve got you covered.

Our Story

Sip and Run Café was born from a runner’s search for more than just another coffee shop. Tired of places that only served drinks, our founder wanted a space that combined strong brews, fresh pastries, and running essentials like shoes, watches, and sunglasses. It’s a café designed for those who chase miles and moments, one sip at a time.
