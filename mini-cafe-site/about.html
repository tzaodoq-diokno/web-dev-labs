Sip and Run Café

Fueling runners and coffee lovers since day one.

Our Origin

Sip and Run Café began with the vision of a runner searching for a place that combined the love of strong coffee, fresh pastries, and essentials for those who run the extra mile. Built in the heart of the city, it became a hub for both casual coffee drinkers and passionate athletes.

Our Values

  • Quality coffee, brewed with care
  • Healthy pastries to fuel your run
  • Community built on passion and perseverance
  • Sustainability in every step
